# Yusho Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2025

## Information We Collect

When you use Yusho, we collect:
- Email address (for account creation)
- Name and profile photo (for matchmaking)
- Sports preferences and skill levels
- Location (only when you grant permission, to show nearby courts)
- Match and chat history
- Device identifiers (for push notifications)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Match you with other players based on sport, skill, and location
- Send you push notifications about matches and games
- Show your profile to potential match partners
- Improve app functionality

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Track you across other apps
- Share your data for advertising

We DO share data with:
- Firebase (Google) for backend infrastructure
- Apple Push Notification Service for notifications

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Access your data via the app
- Delete your account
- Opt out of notifications
- Block other users
